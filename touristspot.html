Welcome to the Philippines

Discover the unparalleled beauty of the Philippines, a tropical paradise filled with breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. From pristine beaches to historical landmarks, there's something here for everyone. Let your adventure begin!

Must-Visit Tourist Spots

San Agustin Church

Located in Intramuros, Manila, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is the oldest stone church in the Philippines, celebrated for its intricate Baroque architecture and historical significance.

Chocolate Hills

Found in Bohol, these geological marvels turn chocolate brown during the dry season and are surrounded by legends that captivate locals and tourists alike.

Banaue Rice Terraces

Hand-carved into the mountains over 2,000 years ago, these terraces in Ifugao Province reflect the ingenuity and resilience of early Filipino communities.

Boracay

Known for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Boracay Island offers a mix of relaxation, water sports, and vibrant nightlife.

El Nido

Located in Palawan, this paradise boasts majestic limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and vibrant marine life, perfect for island-hopping adventures.

Palawan Underground River

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this natural wonder in Puerto Princesa features a navigable underground river flowing through a stunning cave system.

Vigan

A step back in time, Vigan in Ilocos Sur showcases cobblestone streets, ancestral homes, and kalesa rides that reflect its Spanish colonial history.

Cebu

Known as the Queen City of the South, Cebu offers a mix of historical landmarks like Magellan's Cross and stunning beaches.

Siargao

The surfing capital of the Philippines, Siargao is also known for its laid-back vibe, natural rock pools, and stunning palm tree landscapes.

Taal Volcano

A picturesque volcano located within a lake in Batangas, offering stunning views and hiking opportunities.

Mayon Volcano

Renowned for its perfect cone shape, this active volcano in Albay is a sight to behold and a favorite among photographers.

Puerto Galera

A paradise for divers, this destination in Mindoro offers rich coral reefs and vibrant marine life, ideal for snorkeling and diving.

Malapascua Island

Famous for its stunning beaches and as a diving hub to encounter thresher sharks and other unique marine creatures.

Camiguin Island

Also called the "Island Born of Fire," Camiguin is rich in volcanic landscapes, natural springs, and historic landmarks.

Pagudpud

A gem in Ilocos Norte, Pagudpud offers pristine beaches, dramatic windmills, and panoramic ocean views.

Coron

A haven for divers, Coron features breathtaking lagoons, limestone cliffs, and World War II shipwreck dive sites.

Siquijor

This mystical island is famed for its enchanting waterfalls, white sand beaches, and folklore traditions.

Batanes

Known for its dramatic cliffs, rolling hills, and unique stone houses, Batanes offers serenity and breathtaking scenery.

Hundred Islands

This national park in Pangasinan features over a hundred islets, each with unique charm and adventure opportunities.

Enchanted River

Located in Surigao del Sur, this river is famed for its strikingly clear, blue-green waters and mysterious depths.
